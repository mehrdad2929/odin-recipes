Air Fryer Meatloaf

Description

This simple air fryer meatloaf is easy to make and ready to eat in less than an hour.

Ingredients
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 3 tablespoons dry bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 mushrooms, thickly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Preheat an air fryer to 392 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    Combine ground beef, onion, bread crumbs, thyme, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl.

    Knead and mix thoroughly.Transfer mixture to a small loaf pan. Smooth the top,

    press in mushrooms, and coat with olive oil.

  3. Step 3

    Roast meatloaf in the preheated air fryer until nicely browned, about 25 minutes.An

    instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F

    (72 degrees C).

  4. Step 4

    Let meatloaf rest at least 10 minutes before slicing into wedges and serving.
